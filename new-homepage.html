About

Crowdfluttr is a crowdsourcing platform for ideas. We aim to bring the world’s best ideas to life through open collaboration and collective enterprise.

Join our community to plug into the world’s largest brainstorming session, give and receive feedback on amazing ideas, and collaborate with awesome people from around the world.

No idea is too small (or big) when the world is your team.

Together, let's build the world's imagination.




1. Imagine

Pitch ideas to your friends, public figures, or the world at large. We provide the analytics. Every idea matters. Be heard.

2. Validate

Discover and vote for the world's best ideas. Learn which ideas receives the most reach and traction in the market.

3. Collaborate

Collaborate with talented people from around the world to bring the idea to life. Contribute micro-tasks to bring the idea to the next level.

Crowdfluttr is founded by a motley crew of idealists, dreamers, and misfits who believe that every idea deserves a chance to evolve into something greater. If you have an idea too big for you to lift on your own, have no fear. All things are possible when we all work together.

Join Crowdfluttr. Together, let's realize the world's imagination.
